Why this exists

Pricing is slow when the work is not structured

Shops need a repeatable loop: identify the exact card, benchmark from real listings, decide buy/sell, and log it for later. Generic scanning tools don’t enforce that workflow.

Identity ambiguity

Variants, conditions, and edition details get missed under time pressure.

Pricing noise

Outliers, bait listings, and stale posts distort quick “lookups”.

No audit trail

Decisions aren’t logged with sources, notes, or confidence.

Inventory friction

Manual entry doesn’t keep up with real-world intake volume.
