# Design System — TCG Pricer

## Product Context
- **What this is:** Mobile-first web app for scanning trading cards, generating shop-friendly buy/sell suggestions, and logging to Google Sheets.
- **Who it’s for:** Card shops and staff doing fast intake at the counter.
- **Project type:** Mobile web app (PWA-style).

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Direction:** Cozy Storybook
- **Mood:** Warm, crafted, calm, and “small studio” premium. Soft paper surfaces, moss-and-honey accents, gentle light, minimal but soulful.
- **Memorable thing:** Feels magical.

## Typography
- **Display/Hero:** Instrument Serif
- **Body/UI:** Instrument Sans
- **Data/Tables:** Geist Mono (tabular numbers)
- **Scale:**
  - xs: 12px
  - sm: 13px
  - base: 15px
  - lg: 17px
  - xl: 20px
  - 2xl: 26px

## Color
- **Background (paper):** #FBF7EF
- **Surface (card):** #FFFFFF
- **Surface 2 (raised):** #F4EFE6
- **Ink (primary text):** #1E2328
- **Muted text:** #5B5F5A
- **Border:** rgba(33, 38, 34, 0.14)
- **Accent (moss):** #2F6B4F
- **Accent strong:** #21513B
- **Accent 2 (honey):** #D39B37
- **Success:** #2E7D5C
- **Danger:** #B23B3B

## Spacing
- **Base unit:** 4px
- **Density:** Comfortable
- **Scale:** 2xs(4) xs(8) sm(12) md(16) lg(20) xl(28) 2xl(40) 3xl(56)

## Layout
- **Approach:** Hybrid (strict structure + organic warmth)
- **Max content width:** 28rem
- **Radii:**
  - sm: 10px
  - md: 14px
  - lg: 22px
  - xl: 28px

## Motion
- **Approach:** Intentional
- **Duration:** micro(80ms) short(160ms) medium(260ms)
- **Easing:** enter(ease-out) exit(ease-in) move(ease-in-out)
